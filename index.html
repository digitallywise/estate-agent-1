Description

We are pleased to present a fully furnished luxury villa to rent in Haji Real estate situated in Jemo area at the heart of the African capital Addis Ababa. This property is built on 280 square metre with a total area of 600 Square Metre land. It comprises 4 bedrooms, 2 receptions, 3 in one (jacuzzi, steam room and shower). The jaccuzzi room is equipped with TV, telephone line, bluetooth and radio. There are 4 bathrooms, 2 kitchens (modern and traditional), beautiful front and side greenery area and 6 car parking space within the private fence. This property is 2 years old and built with security at the heart and includes cctv, intercom, and fence top electrical wiring for extra security.
